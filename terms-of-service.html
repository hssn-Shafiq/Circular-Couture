If you cancel, you will receive a refund of the Purchase Price in accordance with the refunds policy (see below).

If you purchase a Product from a Seller which is not a Trader Seller; the right to cancel the purchase and obtain a refund of the Purchase Price will be at circularcouture's or the relevant Seller's sole discretion.

To cancel a purchase, you must clearly inform circularcouture (as applicable), preferably:

  • by email to the following address contact@circularcouturecollective.com, giving your name, address and order reference; and
  • by completing and submitting the cancellation form included within the parcel, also available at request on the circularcouture Platform at https://help.circularcouturecollective.com/hc/en-us/requests/new

You must also return the product(s) within 14 days after the day you notify your cancellation, in the same condition in which you receive them (which does not interfere with your right to take any reasonable steps to examine the product(s) and make sure they conform to your order). Products must be returned to the relevant Seller or circularcouture (as applicable). You have a legal obligation to take reasonable care of the product(s) while in your possession. If you fail to comply with this obligation, the relevant Seller may have a right to deduct the cost of any deterioration (due, for example, to your having used the product(s)), up to the price of the product(s), from the refund to which you are otherwise entitled.

To return the product(s), you should package the parcel securely (making sure you include a note of your name and address (enclosing any returns slip, if you have been provided with one) inside the parcel) and then return it to circularcouture/the Seller (as applicable), either by courier or by recorded delivery mail or other form of certified mail or, if the product(s) are too bulky to return by mail, then by a suitable carrier, to the returns address stated in your receipt.

You are advised to take out enough postal/carriage insurance to cover the value of the contents. Please save your proof of posting/despatch and tracking information until your refund has been processed. You will be responsible for the cost and risk of returning the product(s).

Details of the consumer rights described above, and an explanation of how to exercise them, are provided in you receipt. Nothing in this section affects your legal rights.

  1. Refunds policy

If you are a Buyer and you have cancelled your purchase from a Trader Seller, and within the 14-day cooling-off period (see above), any refund due to you will be processed as soon as possible and, in any case, within 14 days after the day on which circularcouture / the relevant Seller receives the product(s) back or, if earlier, the day on which circularcouture / the relevant Seller receive evidence that you have returned the product(s) to the returns address (see above).

You will be refunded the price paid in full (subject to any deduction the relevant Seller is entitled to make due to your use of or damage to the product(s)), including the cost of standard delivery. However, you will not be refunded your cost of returning the product(s). If you received any promotional or other discount when you paid, any refund will only reflect the amount you actually paid. Refunds are made using the same method originally used by you to pay for your purchase, unless agreed otherwise.

If you are a Buyer and you have purchase products from Seller who is not a Trader Seller, your right to return products will be at circularcouture's or the relevant Seller's sole discretion.

All products for sale on the circularcouture Platform are second hand, this means that they may not be in perfect condition. However, if any product that you purchase does not comply with the description and photographs provided by the Seller, you may have one or more legal remedies available to you, depending on when you make the relevant Seller aware of the problem, in accordance with your legal rights.

A product will be considered as not complying with the description and photographs provided by the Seller when the product received in comparison to the description and photographs is: an incorrect size, the wrong colour, a counterfeit item, severely damaged (stains, smells, holes), the wrong condition, incorrect or missing. Conversely, this will not be the cases where the product is not a right fit (but the size was correctly indicated in the listing), or the differences between the product received and the description and photographs only include minor and negligible differences that do not affect the use and look of the product as legitimately expected by the Buyer based on the description and photographs.

If you believe you received a product that does not comply with the description and photographs provided by the Seller, you should inform the relevant Seller as soon as possible, preferably in writing, giving your name, address and order reference.
