How does pricing work?

The rental price of an item is determined by a combination of both the itemâ€™s recommended retail price (RRP) and a day rate which is calculated by our algorithms. The higher the itemâ€™s RRP, the more competitive the day rate becomes. This keeps rental prices fair and accessible on more expensive items. WHAT THESE DATES MEAN?

The Start Date is the date on which you can expect your item to arrive, although it could get to you sooner. If you are renting for an event, we recommend setting the Start Date a few days before.
